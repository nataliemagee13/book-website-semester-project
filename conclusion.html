Conclusion

This project helped answer my research question about how social media shapes reading trends. Based on my research, it is clear that platforms like BookTok and Goodreads have a major impact on what books become popular. They influence both what people see and what they decide to read.

I think this project shows that reading trends today are strongly connected to digital platforms and online communities. At the same time, it also raises new questions, like whether social media limits the variety of books people are exposed to or if it mainly promotes the same popular titles.

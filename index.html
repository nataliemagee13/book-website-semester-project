Welcome!

This site is dedicated to sharing great book recommendations. Whether you enjoy fiction, non-fiction, or everything in between, you'll find something worth reading here. Explore the list below and discover your next favorite book!

Book Recommendations

The Great Gatsby

by F. Scott Fitzgerald

A classic American novel set in the 1920s that explores themes of wealth, class, and the American Dream through the story of the mysterious Jay Gatsby.

To Kill a Mockingbird

by Harper Lee

A powerful story about racial injustice and moral growth in the American South, told through the eyes of young Scout Finch and her father, lawyer Atticus Finch.

1984

by George Orwell

A gripping dystopian novel about a totalitarian society where Big Brother watches every move. A thought-provoking read about freedom, surveillance, and truth.

The Alchemist

by Paulo Coelho

An inspiring tale of a young shepherd named Santiago who goes on a journey to find treasure and discovers the importance of following your dreams.

Educated

by Tara Westover

A remarkable memoir about a woman who grew up in a survivalist family in rural Idaho and went on to earn a PhD from Cambridge University despite never attending school.

The Midnight Library

by Matt Haig

A moving and uplifting novel about a woman who finds herself in a library between life and death, where she can try out different lives she could have lived.

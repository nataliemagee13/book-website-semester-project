BookTok & Social Media's Impact

Social media platforms like BookTok (TikTok's book community) and Bookstagram have revolutionized how people discover and share books. These communities create vibrant spaces where readers recommend titles, discuss themes, and build enthusiasm around literature. The influence of these platforms has made certain books bestsellers overnight and given underrated gems a second chance at recognition.

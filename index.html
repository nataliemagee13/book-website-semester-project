How Social Media Shapes Reading Trends

This project explores how social media shapes reading trends, especially through platforms like BookTok and Goodreads. Today, many people discover books online through recommendations, reviews, and viral trends. This website combines book recommendations with research to better understand how digital platforms influence what people read.

Featured Book Recommendations

Atomic Habits

Author: James Clear

A practical guide to building good habits and breaking bad ones. This book has become a bestseller partly due to social media recommendations and BookTok visibility.

The Midnight Library

Author: Matt Haig

A thought-provoking novel about choices and second chances that gained popularity through Goodreads ratings and BookTok videos before becoming a mainstream hit.

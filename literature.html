Literature Review

Research shows that social media plays a big role in shaping reading trends. Platforms like TikTok, especially BookTok, have made certain books extremely popular through short videos and recommendations. Studies also show that Goodreads influences what people read through ratings, reviews, and algorithm-based suggestions.

At the same time, researchers point out that these platforms don't just reflect what people like but they actually influence what people choose to read by making certain books more visible. This means reading trends are shaped by both user behavior and the design of the platforms themselves.

Analysis & Findings

Through my research, I found that social media has a really strong influence on reading trends. Platforms like TikTok, especially BookTok, can make certain books go viral very quickly. When people keep seeing the same book recommended over and over, they are more likely to read it.

I also noticed that Goodreads plays a big role in shaping what people read through ratings, reviews, and suggested books. These systems make some books more visible than others, which influences what becomes popular.
